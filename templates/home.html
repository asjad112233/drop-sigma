How It Works

From order to delivery — fully managed

1

Connect Your Stores

Link your WooCommerce or Shopify stores. Orders sync in real time via webhooks — no manual imports, no delays.

2

Assign Vendors & Team Members

Set permanent product-to-vendor rules so new orders are auto-assigned. Or manually assign any order to a vendor or team member.

3

Vendor Submits Tracking

Your vendor logs into their portal and submits a tracking number. You review it in the Tracking Queue and approve with one click.

4

Customer Gets Notified

The moment tracking is approved, an automated email fires to the customer with their shipment details — using your branded template.

5

Handle Support with AI

Customer replies land in your Gmail inbox inside Drop Sigma. AI drafts a context-aware reply in seconds — you just review and send.
