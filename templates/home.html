How It Works

From order to happy customer
in 5 automated steps

1
Connect Your Stores
Link WooCommerce or Shopify. Orders sync in real time via webhooks — no manual imports, no delays.
2
Auto-Assign to Vendors
Set permanent product-to-vendor rules. New orders route to the right vendor automatically, every time.
3
Vendor Submits Tracking
Vendors log into their portal and submit a tracking number. You review and approve with one click in the queue.
4
Customer Gets Notified
Approval triggers a branded shipping email to the customer instantly — automatically, with no manual action.
5
AI Handles Support
Customer replies land in your Gmail inbox inside Drop Sigma. AI drafts a context-aware reply in seconds.
