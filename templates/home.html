Frequently asked

Questions, answered.

How long does it take to connect my store? +
Under 2 minutes. Paste your WooCommerce or Shopify API key and secret, hit save, and Drop Sigma fetches your existing orders + sets up live webhooks for new orders automatically.
Does Drop Sigma really update WooCommerce status automatically? +
Yes. Once a tracking number is approved, our headless browser polls the courier's site, parses the live status, and writes the update straight back to your WooCommerce order. Status keywords like "delivered", "in transit", and "out for delivery" are detected and mapped automatically.
Can vendors see my customers' email addresses? +
Only if you let them. Each vendor has granular permission toggles for customer email, order amount, store URL, assigned team member, and more. Defaults are privacy-first.
Does the AI Email Draft actually understand my orders? +
Yes — that's the point. When you click "AI Draft", we feed the model the linked order details (number, product, status, tracking) along with your tone/language settings. The reply that comes back is tailored to that specific situation.
What if I have more than 3 stores? +
Drop Sigma Pro includes 3 stores and unlimited team members. If you need additional stores beyond the 3 included, reach out to us — we can accommodate larger setups.
Is there a free trial? +
We offer coupon codes for discounts. Sign up, and if you have a coupon code you can apply it at checkout to reduce or eliminate the first month's cost.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes. There's no contract. Cancel from your billing settings any time and you keep access until the end of your billing cycle.
